=====Software and Tools=====
[[SPM]] Statistical Parametric Mapping for different types of neuro data \\
[[FSL]] A unix based tool for neuroimaging analysis \\
[[AFNI]] I've only used it for the AlphaSim tool \\
[[conn]] A toolbox for resting connectivity analysis based in MATLAB \\
[[xjView]] A simple tool for displaying images and overlays \\ 
[[xvfb]] Allows sending graphical output to spot in memory, useful for graphical functionality in cluster environment \\

[[http://rsl.stanford.edu/research/software.html|Rdgehdr]] Used to convert pfiles into text to read header info! \\
[[http://www.sph.sc.edu/comd/rorden/mricron/install.html|MRICron]] a nice tool to have for looking quickly at data, and figuring out orientation.  It comes with a package called [[http://www.sph.sc.edu/comd/rorden/mricron/dcm2nii.html|dcm2nii]] that converts DICOM-->NIFTII as well.
